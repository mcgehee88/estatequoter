Meet the EstateQuoter team

EstateQuoter was created by two people who have been inside estate situations personally and professionally. The goal is simple: give families a clear, calm way to get help with estates without pressure, confusion, or endless phone calls.

T
Todd Osborne
Co-Founder • Estate transitions & local network

Todd is a U.S. military veteran and owner of Caring Transitions of Chesapeake & Virginia Beach West, where he has personally helped hundreds of families with estate sales, cleanouts, downsizing, and senior moves.

He brings on-the-ground experience from walking properties, understanding how full they really are, and talking face-to-face with families who are overwhelmed, grieving, or unsure where to start.

Todd and his wife Lynn are long-time members of the Hampton Roads community and care deeply about serving seniors, veterans, and local families with respect and patience.

M
Michael McGehee
Co-Founder • Product & family experience

Based in Virginia Beach, Michael has spent more than a decade building consumer-focused platforms that connect people with the right services in a clear, honest way.

He is also the founder of FreeTrials.com, a directory for software introductory offers, where he designed systems that prioritize trust, transparency, and ease of use.

Michael’s interest in estate services comes from real family experiences with downsizing, inherited homes, and whole-house cleanouts—and wanting that process to feel less overwhelming for other families.
