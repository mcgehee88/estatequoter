Questions estate companies usually ask

Can I use EstateQuoter Pro with my own website and referrals?

Yes. Many companies use EstateQuoter Pro as their main intake system. You can send all prospects through the same link, whether they find you through us, Google, referrals, or social media.

Do I have to commit to a long contract?

No. You can start simple and adjust over time. We want you to feel like the value is obvious, not something you tolerate because of a contract.

How many partners will you have in my area?

For Regional Partner agreements, we keep the number of companies in a given area limited and clear. We are happy to talk through how this would look in your city.
