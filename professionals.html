How it works for you

One intake, less back-and-forth

Families answer a clear set of questions about the property, how full it is, and their timeline. We send those details to you in a single, organized summary, so you spend less time chasing basic info and more time deciding if it is a fit.

What you receive from each request

  • Property basics (ZIP, type, square footage, beds/baths)
  • How full the home is and any extra spaces (garage, attic, basement, sheds)
  • Notes about high-value or specialty items
  • Timeline, access notes, and any tricky items (pianos, safes, hot tubs, etc.)
  • Contact info plus any extra context the family wants you to know
Using EstateQuoter with your own clients

Share your own link with families

If you already work with EstateQuoter, you can send families a direct link to the same intake flow you see on our homepage, but tied to you as the pro.

Example: Todd can tell a family, “Fill this out and I will review it,” and send them a custom URL like estatequoter.com/?pro=todd. Their answers show up on his side with his name attached.

We will set up your pro slug and routing with you directly so it stays simple.
