How EstateQuoter fits into your business

We are not an estate sale company. EstateQuoter is an intake layer that sits between families who need help and professionals like you who can do the work.

  • Families fill out a short, plain-language questionnaire about the home.
  • We send the full details to one or more local partners in that market.
  • You decide if the job is a fit and follow up directly with the family.

Because the questions are structured, you see square footage, fullness, access issues, timeline and special items up front instead of trying to gather it through three different phone calls.

EstateQuoter is designed by people who have worked inside estate sales and cleanouts, so the questions are written to respect families and your crew’s time.
What you get
Leads that feel like warm referrals

When you receive a lead from EstateQuoter, you get:

  • Contact info and notes from the family.
  • Property basics: type, size, beds/baths, extra spaces.
  • Fullness level and any high-value or difficult items.
  • Timeline and clear-out expectations.

That makes it easier to estimate labor, hauling, and sale potential before you commit a truck and a full team to a walkthrough.

How we charge

We charge estate professionals, not families. Most partners start with a simple lead arrangement. If you close business from EstateQuoter leads and want more volume, we can talk about a deeper placement.

What to expect

Simple, no-nonsense partnership

We keep things straightforward: clear expectations, clear geography, and no pressure to take on jobs that are not a fit.

Do you sell our data or share it widely?
No. Leads are shared with a small number of local professionals we’ve agreed to work with in that market. The goal is to connect families with a good match, not blast their info everywhere.
Can we limit which leads we see?
Yes. We can narrow by geography, job type (full estate sale vs. cleanout only), and other basics so you are only seeing jobs that make sense for your crew.
What markets are you active in right now?
We are starting in Virginia Beach and the broader Hampton Roads area, and are adding additional markets cautiously so we do not over-promise to families or to professionals.
