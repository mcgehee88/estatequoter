How it works for your business

Instead of texting back and forth for weeks, you send one link. The client fills it out, EstateQuoter applies your rules, and you decide what to take and what to pass on.

Step 1

Set your rules once

Tell EstateQuoter how you price jobs, what size homes you accept, what areas you cover, and which jobs are an automatic no. You control your own margin.

Step 2

Share your link

Send people to estatequoter.com/yourname from your site, email signature, business cards, or referral partners. Every lead lands in the same, clean format.

Step 3

Collect photos and details

Families answer your pre-built questions, upload pictures, and share timing, access, and special situations so you are never walking in blind again.

Step 4

Review the estimate and reply fast

EstateQuoter gives you a structured summary and a suggested range based on your rules. You decide the final quote, send it, and track if the job is won or not.

Questions pros ask before they sign up

Is this replacing my website?
No. EstateQuoter sits next to your website. You can link to it from your site, social profiles, email signature, and referral partners so every job starts in the same clean intake flow.
Do you send me new clients or just organize my leads?
Both. Every plan gives you a private intake link for your own leads. Paid plans can also appear in the EstateQuoter directory so families can discover you directly when they request quotes.
Can I control what kinds of jobs I see?
Yes. You set your service area, home size ranges, timing, and other filters so you spend less time on jobs that are too small, too far away, or not a fit.
What happens after someone fills out my form?
You receive a structured summary by email, and in paid plans it also appears in your light dashboard. From there you can review the details, adjust the estimate, and follow up with the family.
