How EstateQuoter Pro works

We’re building this with real operators who live inside estate sales and cleanouts every day. The goal is simple: less chaos, fewer back-and-forth messages, and better-fit jobs.

Step 1

You tell us how you work

On a short onboarding call, we capture your service areas, ideal job profile, deal breakers, fee structures, and what you want families to know up front.

Step 2

We set up your intake link

We configure your branded intake link (for example estatequoter.com/yourname), tuned to your rules and language so it feels like an extension of your company.

Step 3

Families fill it out once

They answer a clean sequence of questions: property type, square footage, how full it is, photos, timeline, special items, and access details. You see everything in one place.

Step 4

You review, or let rules send an estimate

You can manually review every request or choose simple rules that allow a ballpark range to be emailed when the job clearly fits your model.

Step 5

Optional: EstateQuoter leads

In markets where we have family traffic, we can also route EstateQuoter requests that match your filters directly into your dashboard.
