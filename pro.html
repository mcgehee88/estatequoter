Questions pros often ask

How the link, leads, and quote rules fit together

Is the intake link still useful if I never use the lead program?
Yes. The intake link is valuable on its own. Many companies start by using it just for their existing referrals and clients. You can treat Pro features like a second step for when you are ready to grow.
Who owns the relationship with the families who fill out the form?
When someone uses your intake link they are reaching you. Pro features simply help route and organize those requests. If we send you an EstateQuoter lead that matches your profile we are clear about how that handoff works.
Do I have to change my whole website to use this?
No. You can add your intake link as a single “Get a quote” or “Tell us about the estate” button. Many teams start by giving the link to a few referral partners or adding it to email signatures before changing anything else.
How do quote rules work in practice?
Quote rules do not guess at prices for you. They apply your own guardrails to the answers in the form so your team can see which jobs likely fit your ideal window and which ones may need a different approach.
