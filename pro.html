Questions from pros

A few things we get asked early in the conversation.
Is this just a generic “lead selling” site?

No. EstateQuoter started as a way to give families a calmer starting point when they are staring at a full house and do not know who to call first. EstateQuoter Pro exists because the best companies kept telling us they wanted cleaner, more complete information before they committed a crew.

Do we have to change how we price or talk to clients?

No. We do not script your calls or force a specific pricing model. Our job is to help families explain the situation clearly, then let you run your business the way that works for you.

How many other pros see the same request?

EstateQuoter does not blast every request to an endless list of companies. We keep the number of Pro partners limited in each area so that families are not overwhelmed and pros are not competing with dozens of lookalike responses.

What does it cost to be in EstateQuoter Pro?

We review every application one by one and talk through pricing and structure based on your service area and how you want to use EstateQuoter (leads, your own intake link, or both). There are no surprise add-ons buried in the fine print.
