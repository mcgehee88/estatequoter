Questions pros often ask

How the intake link, listing, and leads work together

Is this mainly for listing my company or for intake?
Intake comes first. Every company starts by getting a custom link they can use with their own clients and referrals. Listing and leads are optional tools we layer on later if they make sense for your business.
Who owns the relationship with the families who fill out the form?
When someone uses your intake link, they are contacting you. EstateQuoter is the bridge. If a request comes from the EstateQuoter site, we are clear about which company it is being sent to and why.
Do I have to switch my whole website or CRM to use this?
No. You can start with a single “Tell us about the estate” or “Get a quote” button that points to your intake link. You can keep using whatever email and systems you already have behind the scenes.
How do quote rules work in real life?
You tell us your deal breakers and sweet spots. For example distance from your base, size of the estate, and timing. We use those settings to flag which requests are likely a good match. You still decide what to charge and how to respond.
