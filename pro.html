How EstateQuoter Pro works

EstateQuoter does not replace your website. It gives you a clean intake flow on top of what you already have. Families use your link, and you get better information with less work.

Step 1

Set your rules once

You tell us what you handle and what you avoid. Things like property size, distance, type of work and timing.

Step 2

Share your link

Add your link to your website, email signature, business cards, and share it with realtors, attorneys and referral partners.

Step 3

Families submit details and media

They answer a short intake, mark how full the home is, note access issues and add photos or a quick walkthrough video.

Step 4

Review, quote and track

You receive a structured summary in your inbox and Pro dashboard. Mark jobs as won, lost or pending and keep everything in one place.
